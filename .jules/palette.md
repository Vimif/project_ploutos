## 2024-03-20 - Dashboard Filter Group Accessibility
**Learning:** Dashboard form elements within `filter-group` containers across various templates lack proper explicit `for` attribute linkages and visual cues for interactivity, hindering screen reader usability and clickability on labels.
**Action:** When working on form filters, always ensure explicit `for` attributes on `<label>` tags linked to the `id` of their respective `input` or `select` elements, and include `cursor: pointer` to indicate interactivity. Elements without explicit text must use `aria-label` to ensure screen reader accessibility.
